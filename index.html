ABOUT US

National Commission for Human Development is a government organization whose mission is to improve access to basic education and healthcare in the country's poorest communities. The contribution of NCHD is tremendous and the organization won UNESCO Prize for the promotion of education in Pakistan, especially under-served areas. NCHD is working in 134 districts of Pakistan.
